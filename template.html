Info

Prices shown are in USD and based on the most recent available data. Rarer cards may not have been on the market recently. Games highlighted red are missing market data and show a cost extrapolated from the prices of available cards.

"Discount" indicates how much money you would make selling your drops for a game, at low market price and after Valve's 15% cut, and assuming no foils.
